About Transformers

Transformers are intelligent alien robots from the planet Cybertron. They possess the incredible ability to transform into vehicles, aircraft, weapons, and many other machines.

The Transformers universe revolves around the battle between two powerful factions: the heroic Autobots, led by Optimus Prime, and the evil Decepticons, commanded by Megatron.

Autobots

Protectors of Earth

The Autobots fight for freedom, peace, and justice. Their leader, Optimus Prime, inspires courage and teamwork while protecting humanity from evil.

Decepticons

Masters of Destruction

Led by Megatron, the Decepticons seek power and domination. They are powerful warriors who use fear, strength, and advanced technology to achieve their goals.
